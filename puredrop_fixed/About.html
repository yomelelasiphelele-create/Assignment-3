About PureDrop Foundation

A non-profit organisation committed to ending the water crisis, one community at a time.

Who We Are

PureDrop Foundation is a South African non-profit organisation focused on improving lives through access to safe and clean drinking water.

Water Changes Everything

Millions of people still lack access to clean drinking water. Our mission is to build sustainable water systems, educate communities, and create a healthier future for children and families across South Africa.


Through partnerships, donations, and volunteer programmes, we help communities gain reliable access to safe water every day — reducing waterborne illness, improving school attendance, and empowering women and girls who no longer spend hours collecting water.
