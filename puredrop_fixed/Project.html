Our Water Projects

See how PureDrop Foundation is improving lives through clean water initiatives across South Africa.

Village Borehole Installation

Installed sustainable boreholes that provide clean water to rural families and schools in the Eastern Cape.

Clean Water Distribution

Delivered purified drinking water to communities affected by drought and water shortages in the Northern Cape.

Water Safety Workshops

Educated families and learners about hygiene, water purification, and sanitation practices.

Community Water Tanks

Installed water storage tanks to improve access to clean water in remote communities across Limpopo.

School Water Programme

Supplied schools with safe drinking water systems for healthier learning environments in KwaZulu-Natal.

Volunteer Outreach

Volunteers helped distribute water filters and support local clean water campaigns in rural areas.
